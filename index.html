About

Meet Matthew

I’m a mission-driven educator with a passion for equity and lifelong learning. Over the past decade I’ve taught English as a Second Language and developed curricula across four continents—from elementary classrooms in Indonesia to adult-education and college prep in Oregon.

I hold a BA in Cultural Anthropology, an MS in Special Education, and Oregon teaching licensure (SPED & Foundational Math). My goal: help every learner overcome barriers, build confidence, and achieve their academic goals.
